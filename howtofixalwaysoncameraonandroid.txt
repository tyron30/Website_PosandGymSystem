Option 1 — Chrome flag workaround (fastest, works today, Android only)
On each Android phone/tablet using Chrome, you can manually whitelist your server IP as a trusted secure origin:

Open Chrome on the device
Go to chrome://flags/#unsafely-treat-insecure-origin-as-secure
In the text box, enter: http://192.168.0.243
Set the dropdown next to it to Enabled
Tap Relaunch at the bottom
Reopen your scanner URL
